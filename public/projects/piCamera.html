Raspberry Pi Camera

The camera is the first addition to the Smart Life family since the capstone project ended. The goal of this device is to take the origional capstone devices running on a Raspberry Pi Zero W and integrate the 'Pi Camera' with as many practical features as possible. A list of these features is given below:

Single Shot Mode

Signle shot mode takes a single photo and either saves it to the device, saves it to a usb drive, or e-mails the photo to a preset e-mail address.

Time Lapse Mode

The user defines the amount of hours for the time lapse and the photos per hour. This information is used to create a series of photos which is stitched together into an mp4 video. The video is either saved to the device or to an external usb drive. All photos are deleted once the video is taken.

Video Mode

Video mode takes a video.

Security Mode

The device utilizes a motion sensor and will take a photo in a set ineteval so long as motion is detected. This photos are sent to the predefined e-mail as soon as they are taken.

Potential additions include a thermal sensor (for enhanced tracking), the ability to record video or take photos at will from a remote location, etc...

Video Door Bell Mode

The device can be attached to the eyepiece of a door and can act as a video door bell.
