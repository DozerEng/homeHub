Sudoku ( C )

Online version coming soon, offline version is available now

This game of sudoku was inspired by a Computerphile video on youtube where they used a recursive algorithm to solve sudokus. Before I watched the video I attempted to write a program that solved sudokus in a deductive manor. Hundreds of lines later, I chose to watch the video and was amazed but the simpilicity and power of the algorithm Computerphile used.
